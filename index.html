The Lim Name

Exploring the verified linguistic and cultural history of the surname Lim

Character Analysis

The Chinese character 林 (Lim) is composed of two 木 (wood/tree) characters. This is a pictographic representation of multiple trees, meaning "forest" or "woods."

Key facts about the character:

  • 木 Single tree radical
  • 林 Two trees forming "forest"

Character Classification

The character 林 is classified as an associative compound (会意) in the traditional Six Categories of Chinese Characters.
